What is Information Technology (IT)?

Information Technology (IT) refers to the use of computers, networks, and digital systems to process, store, and share information. It plays a vital role in every aspect of modern life, from business and communication to education and entertainment.

The IT field includes several key areas such as:

  • Networking: Building and maintaining computer networks that connect people and devices.
  • Software Development: Creating applications, websites, and systems that solve real-world problems.
  • Cybersecurity: Protecting systems and data from unauthorized access or attacks.
  • Data Science: Analyzing and visualizing data to make smarter decisions.
  • Cloud Computing: Managing and deploying services and resources through the internet.
